Umberto's Ravioli

Description

A recipe of complete grandeur! From the streets of Milano, This is the most authentic recipe for Ravioli in the World! Named after the famous chef Roberto Umberto this has been an Italiano favorite for generations.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Dough
  • flour
  • Homemade red sauce
  • Basil
  • Parsley
  • Mushrooms
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • Salt, Pepper, Crushed Red Pepper
