After Pasta Limoncello

Mamma Mia!! This after dinner shot of limoncello will have you feeling good and have your digestion working good too!

This recipe is made up of all natural ingredients and can be enjoyed anytime but is used traditionally after meals.

Ingredients

  • Lemons
  • Pure Grain Alcohol 190 proof
  • Water
  • White Sugar

Steps

  1. Prepare Lemon Peels & Infuse Alcohol
  2. Place all the lemon peels in the jar with alcohol.
  3. Leave to macerate for at least 8 days, best 3 weeks.
  4. Prepare Sugar Syrup
  5. Mix Lemon Infused Alcohol & Sugar Syrup To Make Limoncello
