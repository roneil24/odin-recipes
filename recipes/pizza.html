New York Style Pizza

Description

This is a simple recipe that will take your taste buds straight to the bustling streets of Manhattan.

A quick recipe consisting of fresh dough made from scratch, fresh mozzarella, and a red sauce probably made by a guy named Giuseppe.

Ingredients

  • fresh pizza dough
  • fresh mozzarella
  • Red Sauce
  • fresh basil
  • flour

Steps

  1. Take the flour and spread it on the counter
  2. Roll the dough in the flour and start neading it into a circular shape. Make it to preferred size.
  3. Spread red sauce evenly on the flattened dough
  4. Once sauce is spread, cut mozzarella and distribute around pizza to preference.
  5. Preheat oven to 425 and Cook pizza for 15-20 minutes.
  6. When the pizza is cooked, garnish with basil. Cut and Serve.
